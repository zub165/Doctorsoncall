Delete your account & data

Doctor On Call / Docs On Call

Option 1 — In the app (recommended)

  1. Sign in to the app.
  2. Open Settings (drawer menu).
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account and linked data are removed from our servers. Cancel Apple subscriptions separately under Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Option 2 — Email request

Email info@innovatorsgeneration.com from the address you used in the app. Include:

  • Your account email or username
  • The message: Please delete my Docs On Call account and associated data.

We may verify your identity before processing. Most requests complete within 7–30 days.

What is deleted

  • Account profile (name, email, phone where stored)
  • Patient or provider profile linked to your user
  • Appointments, documents, and records tied to your account, where permitted by law

Retention

Some data may be kept when required for legal, security, or billing disputes. We will explain any retention if it applies.
