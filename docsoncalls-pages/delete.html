Doctor On Call (EMR)

Contact email: info@innovatorsgeneration.com

Use the steps below to request deletion of your account and associated data. We may request verification to protect your privacy.

How to request deletion

  1. Email info@innovatorsgeneration.com from the email you used in the app.
  2. Include your account email/username and write: Please delete my Doctor On Call account and associated data.
  3. If you have multiple accounts, list each email/username to delete.

What will be deleted

  • Account profile details associated with your user.
  • Uploaded documents/attachments associated with your account.
  • Appointment records associated with your account, where legally permitted.

Processing time

Most requests are processed within 7–30 days, depending on verification and legal requirements.

Data retention exceptions

Some data may be retained when required by law, for security/audit, or to resolve disputes. If retention applies, we will explain what is retained and why.
